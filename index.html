What We Do

Built for Cleveland. Built to Last.

Driveways & Patios

Custom poured slabs with proper drainage, air-entrained mix, and salt-resistant sealers — engineered for Cleveland winters.

Restoration & Repair

Save thousands vs. full replacement. We resurface, crack-inject, and seal deteriorated concrete so it looks brand new again.

Stamped & Decorative

50+ pattern options including flagstone, brick, and wood plank. Color integral or acid-stained to match any home's aesthetic.
